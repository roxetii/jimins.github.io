Enerverse (2024)


Enerverse is a top down 2D RPG that was a contest entry for Energy Creates 2024. You get to play as Millie, a curious girl who can talk with animals! She gets to learn about the Canadian energy sector by exploring the world and chatting with whoever she finds.
